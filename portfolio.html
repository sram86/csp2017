Under this section of my webpage, you will be able to find some of the work I was able to create in class!

This is a remix my group and I were able to create. We added a score keeper that keeps track of how many points the user is able to gain. We also incorporated another paddle to the game to make it more challenging, and a timer that stops the game after 35 seconds. We were able to make the balls say, "Times up!" after the 35 seconds, and make the score tracker stop tracking the score after the time is up. In addition, the background changes to a brick wall when the time is up.
